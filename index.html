About

Reconditioned, not repainted.

Team Intl Imports buys wholesale, reconditions in-house, and sells at retail. Every car leaves the service bay with fresh fluids, current tires, and a Carfax printed and clipped to the window. We don't mark up for mark-up's sake.

Richard Rivera and the crew handle sourcing, service, and sale personally. If something isn't right, we don't sell it. Ask for the inspection sheet — we'll print one.
